Jaguar

Unlike most other cat's, Jaguars love the water and are very strong swimmers.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca
  • Average Size: 100 to 250 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 12 to 15 years
  • Habitat: Rainforest

    • Jaguars are the third biggest cat in the world after tigers and lions, They have the Strongest Bite of the Cat Kingdom, allowing them to bite straight through the skull of it's prey. They resemble leopards, which live in Africa and Asia, but jaguars’ spots are more complex and often have a dot in the center.
